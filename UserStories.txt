Functional Requirement User Stories:
1. As a patient, I want to know how much insulin is needed, so that I can stay healthy.
2. As a patient, I want to know when I have little sugar, so that I can go get more sugar for my body.
3. As a patient, I want to know when my sugar is fine, so that I know my body is doing well.
4. As a patient, if the sensor picks up high blood sugar, I want the product to deliver the insulin to me, so that I don't have to visit the hospital. 
5. As a patient, I want the product to stop administering insulin if I remove the pump so that insulin is not wasted, and I am not over-medicated when I reattach the pump.
6. As a patient, I want the product to display a message, so that I am aware of what is going on with my glucose levels.
7. As a patient, I want the product to contact emergency services if I am unable to respond to warning messages so that my life will be saved.
8. As a patient, I want my data to have the option of being uploaded online, so that I can access the information with ease and track it at any time.
8. As a doctor, I want to be able to access my patient's insulin pump information, so that I can ensure his/her safety and instruct my patient on better use of the pump.
9. As a patient, I want child-lock options for my insulin pump so that my children are not able to mess with it and harm themselves or myself doing so.
10.As a patient, I want to be able to tell the pump if I have administered a certain amount of insulin to myself by other means than the pump so that the pump does not give me too much insulin.
11. As a patient, I want the product to regulate the temperature of the insulin so that it does not go bad due to having too high or too low of a temperature.
11. As a patient, I want the product to have a custom mode available, so that I am not given more insulin than what is needed at the given time

Non-Functional Requirement User Stories:
1. As an administrator, I want this project to be coded in Java and JavaFX, so my team can maintain it easily.
2. As a user, I want this machine to maintain my blood sugar, or else I am in risk of death.
3. As an administrator, I want this project's database to be coded in MySQL, because my team can maintain it easier.
